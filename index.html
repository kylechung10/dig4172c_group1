Humanti

Welcome to Humanti, an online platform being built to spread awareness about the increasing effects of plastic usage. Our goal is to provide users with all the information they need in order to maintain an eco-friendly lifestyle. Humanti encourages users to get involved in the environmental crisis that is plastic pollution. There are a ton of things we can do as individuals to help make the world a cleaner place and it starts right at home!

Did You Know?

  • Did you know that around 8.3 billion tons of plastic have been produced worldwide?
  • Did you know that plastic usage in some parts of the world is illegal?
  • Did you know that 73% of beach litter worldwide is plastic?
  • Did you know a million plastic bottles are bought around the world every minute?

Tips

  • Bring useable bags to the grocery store
  • Recycle any plastics you may have at home
  • Participate in local beach clean-ups
  • Support us and other organizations addressing plastic pollution
